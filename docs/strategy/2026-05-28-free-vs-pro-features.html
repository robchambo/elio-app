Elio — Free vs Pro

What every cook gets for free, and what unlocks with Pro. Ordered the way you move through the app — from your first recipe to feeding the whole household.

Reflects shipped behaviour as of build S17--28may-c (28 May 2026). Source of truth: EntitlementService. Free generation cap = 7/week; saved-recipe history cap = 20.

Free Pro unlocks
01

Generate recipes

Free
  • 7 AI recipes a week, built around what's actually in your pantry
  • Allergen- & dietary-safe (hard constraints honoured)
  • Pantry mode & Go Wild mode
  • Craving, mood, style, time & meal-type controls
  • Regenerate / "Generate another" (counts toward the weekly 7)
  • Ingredient swap — long-press any ingredient to substitute
Pro unlocks
  • Unlimited recipe generation — no weekly cap
  • Suggest a Side Dish — complementary dish for any recipe
02

Your pantry

Free — full feature
  • 3-tier pantry (always have / almost always / perishable)
  • Pantry Builder, custom items, "your usuals"
  • Expiry tracking with colour-coded freshness
  • Running-low flags + Restock bridge to shopping
  • Barcode & receipt scanning straight into the pantry
Pro unlocks
  • Nothing held back — the pantry is fully free.
03

Cook

Free — full feature
  • Hands-free Cook Mode with voice control
  • Inline cooking timers + screen-stays-on
  • Step-by-step method view, serving scaler
Pro unlocks
  • Cooking is fully free.
04

Save & organise recipes

Free
  • Save & bookmark recipes
  • Collections / tags, "makeable now" filter
  • Recipe library up to 20 saved recipes
Pro unlocks
  • Unlimited saved-recipe library — no 20-recipe ceiling
05

Bring your own recipes

Free
  • Importing external recipes is a Pro feature.
Pro unlocks
  • Recipe Import — photo scan, paste a URL, or type one in
  • Email-forward order import — forward a grocery order, items land in your pantry
06

Plan your week

Free
  • The weekly meal planner is a Pro feature.
Pro unlocks
  • 7-day meal planner — breakfast / lunch / dinner generation
  • Per-slot regenerate, swipe between days
07

Shopping list

Free
  • The shopping list is a Pro feature.
Pro unlocks
  • Auto shopping list from any recipe or the whole meal plan
  • Pantry-aware (skips what you already have)
  • Aisle grouping, quantity consolidation, share sheet
08

Batch & bulk

Free
  • Bulk Prep is a Pro feature.
Pro unlocks
  • Bulk Prep mode — batch-cook multiple meals × portions
  • Freezing / reheating / storage guidance per recipe
09

Household

Free
  • Your own profile — dietary needs & allergens
  • Owner only (1 person)
Pro unlocks
  • Up to 6 household members
  • Combined dietary & allergen union across everyone

Not signed in? (Guest mode)
You can try Elio without an account — 3 recipes a week, device-local. Sign in to lift it to the free 7 a week, sync your profile, and keep your recipes. Pro removes the cap entirely and unlocks everything above.
